## Data Storage and Security

### 1. Local Storage
Your watchlists, alerts, and cached market data are stored locally on your device using:
- Room database (SQLite)
- Encrypted local storage
- Secure SharedPreferences for tokens

### 2. Cloud Storage
We may store limited data on secure cloud servers:
- Account information (email, name)
- Sync tokens for multi-device access
- Backup of watchlists and alerts (if enabled)

### 3. Security Measures
We implement industry-standard security measures:
- **Encryption in Transit:** All data transmitted between the App and our servers uses HTTPS/TLS
- **Encryption at Rest:** Sensitive data stored locally is encrypted using Android Keystore
- **Biometric Authentication:** Optional fingerprint/face unlock using Android BiometricPrompt API
- **Secure Token Storage:** OAuth tokens encrypted and stored securely
- **No Password Storage:** We use Google Sign-In; no passwords are stored by us

### 4. Data Retention
- **Account Data:** Retained until you delete your account
- **Usage Analytics:** Retained for 24 months, then anonymized
- **Crash Reports:** Retained for 90 days
- **Deleted Accounts:** All personal data deleted within 30 days

## Your Rights and Choices

### 1. Access and Update
You can access and update your account information at any time:
- View your profile in the App
- Update your name and email via Google account settings
- Manage watchlists and alerts directly in the App

### 2. Delete Your Account
You can delete your account at any time:
- Go to Profile > Settings > Delete Account
- All personal data will be deleted within 30 days
- Watchlists, alerts, and preferences will be permanently removed
- Anonymized analytics may be retained

### 3. Opt-Out of Analytics
You can disable analytics collection:
- Go to Profile > Settings > Privacy
- Toggle "Share Usage Data" to OFF
- Note: Crash reports cannot be disabled (essential for app stability)

### 4. Notification Preferences
You can control push notifications:
- Manage alert notifications in Alerts settings
- Disable all notifications in device settings (Settings > Apps > VETTR > Notifications)

### 5. Data Export
You can export your data:
- Contact privacy@vettr.app to request a copy of your data
- We will provide a machine-readable file (JSON) within 30 days

### 6. Data Portability
You have the right to receive your data in a structured, commonly used format:
- Watchlists, alerts, and preferences can be exported
- Contact privacy@vettr.app to request data portability
